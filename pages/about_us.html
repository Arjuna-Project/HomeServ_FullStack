Our Story

HomeServ was founded in 2025 with a simple mission: to make quality home services accessible and reliable for every family in Chennai. What started as a small team of passionate entrepreneurs has grown into the city’s most trusted home services platform.

We recognized that finding reliable professionals for home maintenance and services was a major challenge faced by Chennai residents. Traditional methods were time-consuming, unreliable, and often resulted in poor service quality. That’s when we decided to change the game. We created a technology-driven approach ensuring seamless booking, transparent pricing, and guaranteed service quality.

Our Mission

To revolutionize the home services industry in Chennai by providing a reliable, technology-driven platform that connects homeowners with skilled professionals, ensuring quality service delivery and complete customer satisfaction.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and preferred home services platform across South India, setting new standards for service quality, professional training, and customer experience.
